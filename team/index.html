About Vanguard Realty Group — Corvallis, Oregon

We started Vanguard because real estate deserved better.

Founded in 2015, Vanguard Realty Group is a Corvallis, Oregon-based brokerage serving buyers, sellers, and commercial clients across Corvallis, Albany, Lebanon, and the Willamette Valley. We set out to build a different kind of real estate company — one where clients are treated like people, not a transaction.

"Every person deserves the personal touch. Relationships take time — and they're worth every minute."

We're a small, focused brokerage — and that's by design. Every client gets real attention from someone who knows the local market and will give you a straight answer, whether it's what you want to hear or not.
