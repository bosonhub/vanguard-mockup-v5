Industrial Space in the Heart of the Willamette Valley

The Corvallis–Albany industrial corridor is one of the most strategically positioned in western Oregon. With direct I-5 access, proximity to the Port of Portland, and a growing manufacturing and logistics base, demand for industrial space in this market has remained consistently strong.

We represent buyers, sellers, landlords, and tenants in industrial transactions ranging from small flex spaces to large-scale distribution facilities. Our knowledge of Linn and Benton County zoning, environmental considerations, and off-market opportunities gives our clients a genuine advantage.
