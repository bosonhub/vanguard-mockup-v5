Multi-Family Investment — Corvallis & Albany, Oregon

Why Invest in Willamette Valley Multi-Family?

Corvallis is home to Oregon State University — one of the largest universities in the Pacific Northwest — which creates a consistent, year-round demand for rental housing. Combined with a stable local employment base and limited new construction, the Corvallis multi-family market has historically delivered strong occupancy rates and steady rent growth.

Albany offers a more affordable entry point with strong fundamentals: a growing population, proximity to Corvallis, and a diverse employment base. Both markets are well-suited for long-term buy-and-hold investment strategies.

  • Duplex and triplex properties
  • Small apartment buildings (4–20 units)
  • Student housing near OSU
  • Value-add and stabilized assets
  • 1031 exchange advisory

The Corvallis rental market at a glance

OSU enrollment drives consistent demand for rental housing in Corvallis, with vacancy rates typically running well below state and national averages. The combination of a captive renter population and limited new supply has made this one of the most stable multi-family markets in Oregon.
